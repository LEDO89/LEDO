# Archive

## Purpose

`10_archive/` contains deprecated, superseded, inactive, or historical materials.

This directory is not an active architecture source of truth. Archived materials may provide historical context, review evidence, or rationale, but they must not be treated as current requirements.

Active documents under `AGENTS.md`, `00_master_architecture/`, and the active numbered architecture and specification folders take precedence over anything archived here.

`review_artifacts/` contains historical review outputs and verification notes.

## Notes

Archived files can explain prior decisions or cleanup history. They do not override active architecture, specification, implementation, validation, safety, approval, execution, or audit guidance.
